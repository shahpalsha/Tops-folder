Necessary steps to publish a Flutter app on Google Play Store:-


1) Prepare your flutter app:
   - Test Your App Thoroughly
   - Update Dependencies
   - Build for Release


2) Set up app signing:
   - Generate a Keystore File
   - Configure Signing in Gradle


3) Create a google play developer account:
   - Sign Up at Google Play Console
   - Create a New App


4) Set up play store listing:
   - Fill Out App Details
   - Add Content Rating
   - Set Up Pricing & Distribution


5) Upload and release the app:
   - Upload the AAB File
   - Review and Rollout


6) Post-Publish actions:
   - Monitor Reviews & Fix Issues
   - Update App Regularly
